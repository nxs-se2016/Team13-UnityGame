HOW TO USE THE PRESSURE PLATES:
1. Drag a Pressure Plate prefab in your scene.
2. Under 'Responsivity' in the Pressure Plate, select the tag the Pressure Plate should interact with.
3. Assign functions to the events (On Tag Collision Enter, On Tag Collision Exit, On Tag Collision Stay).
4. You can edit other settings like the Audio and Animations below.
